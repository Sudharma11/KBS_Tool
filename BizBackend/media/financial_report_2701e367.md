# Deloitte: Comprehensive Financial Report

## Executive Summary

Deloitte is the world's largest professional services network, providing audit, consulting, tax, and advisory services. With a global presence in over 150 countries and territories, Deloitte serves a wide range of clients, including nearly 90% of the Fortune 500 companies. This report provides a comprehensive analysis of Deloitte's financial health, competitive landscape, and market position, incorporating recent developments and industry trends.

## Company Overview

### Founding and History

Founded in London in 1845 by William Welch Deloitte, the firm has grown from a humble accountancy practice to a global professional services powerhouse. Deloitte expanded into the United States in 1890 and has since undergone numerous mergers and acquisitions, broadening its service offerings and geographic reach.

### Core Offerings

Deloitte's core offerings include:

*   **Audit & Assurance:** Independent audits and assurance services.
*   **Consulting:** Management, technology, and human capital consulting.
*   **Tax:** Tax planning, compliance, and advisory services.
*   **Advisory:** Financial advisory, risk management, and forensic services.
*   **Legal:** Legal services

### Global Presence and Employee Count

Deloitte operates in over 150 countries and territories and employs approximately 460,000 people worldwide as of 2024.

### Mission and Values

Deloitte's mission is to make an impact that matters for its clients, its people, and society. The firm is committed to delivering high-quality services, fostering a culture of innovation and collaboration, and contributing to the communities in which it operates.

## Financial Analysis

### Revenue and Market Position

Deloitte reported aggregate global revenue of US$67.2 billion for the fiscal year ending May 31, 2024 (FY2024), representing a 3.1% increase in local currency. Deloitte LLP and its subsidiaries recorded $35.7 billion in U.S. revenues in the fiscal year ended May 31, 2025. As the largest professional services network globally, Deloitte holds a leading market position.

### Financial Stability and Funding

Deloitte's substantial revenue indicates strong financial stability. As a private partnership, Deloitte does not seek external funding through public markets. Its operations are funded through revenue generation and partner contributions, allowing for reinvestment in the firm and strategic acquisitions.
